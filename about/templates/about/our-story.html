Our Story

Founded in February 2019, FizzyBeese is a locally-owned business located in Bristol. We specialize in providing a diverse selection of handmade bath products and fragrances, designed to offer moments of relaxation and sensory delight.

Originating against the backdrop of Bristol, FizzyBeese started by offering a modest range of handmade bath bombs. Our offerings have since expanded to encompass a wide variety of highly fragranced wax melts, available in an array of colors, shapes, and scents. Additionally, our product line now extends to include essentials like Soap, Soap Sponges, Body Souffl√©, Car Perfumes, Scented Aroma Beads, and Wax Melt Burners, with more innovations on the horizon.

Beyond our products, FizzyBeese plays an active role in local fairs and charity events, where we set up stalls to showcase our offerings. These occasions provide an opportunity to connect with the community and share our products in person. We believe that these face-to-face interactions are essential in creating lasting connections with our customers and understanding their preferences.

Discover the thoughtfully curated selection from FizzyBeese and join us in embracing moments of tranquility, all while supporting sustainable choices. Our journey is characterized by a fusion of functionality, nature, and community, inviting you to experience a glimpse into the world of FizzyBeese.
